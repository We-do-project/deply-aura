E-Scrap

Sustainable E-Scrap Solutions for a Greener Future

At Aura, we are dedicated to creating a cleaner, more sustainable world through responsible e-waste recycling. We partner with businesses, schools, universities, and organizations to collect and refurbish all types of computer electronics, giving them a second life while reducing environmental impact. We handle a wide range of electronics, including motherboards, processors, RAM, server boards, laptops, desktops, monitors, televisions, audio mixers, and more. Our goal is to recycle, refurbish, and redistribute these valuable resources to non-profit organizations and communities in need, making a positive impact on both the environment and society.

What We do??

E-Scrap Collection & Recycling
We offer customized solutions for businesses, educational institutions, and organizations looking to responsibly dispose of their computer electronics. Whether you have individual components like motherboards or complete devices like laptops and desktops, we make the recycling process easy, secure, and eco-friendly.
Refurbishment for Reuse
Once collected, your electronic devices are given a second life through our professional refurbishment process. We restore computers, monitors, and peripherals to near-new condition, ready for resale to non-profits and communities. This helps reduce e-waste, extend the lifespan of valuable electronics, and provide affordable technology to those who need it most.
Eco-Friendly Disposal
For items that can't be refurbished, we ensure that all components are responsibly recycled following the highest environmental standards. This process helps prevent harmful materials from ending up in landfills, preserving the ecosystem for future generations.
